# Research references

UI Forge was informed by static research into the following open-source repositories. Upstream project identifiers are recorded at topic level in `extraction-map.csv`, not on individual catalog entries. This record documents research inputs; it does not indicate verbatim copying, inclusion of upstream material, affiliation, or endorsement.

The project does not include or execute source-repository installers, CLIs, servers, image scripts, persistence tools, or generated asset bundles. The retained license texts and revision records are conservative research records, not a claim that upstream code, prose, data, or assets are bundled with UI Forge. See `AUDIT.md` for the review scope, results, and limitations.
